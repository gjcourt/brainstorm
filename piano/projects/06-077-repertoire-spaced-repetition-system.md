# Repertoire Spaced Repetition System

## Description
Build an app to manage your active repertoire. Instead of flashcards, the "items" are pieces or specific sections of pieces. The app schedules when you should review a piece to ensure it stays in your muscle memory, based on your self-reported performance quality.
