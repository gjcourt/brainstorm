# Interactive Circle of Fifths Explorer

## Description

Create an interactive, visual representation of the Circle of Fifths. Allow clicking on a key to
highlight its relative minor, dominant, subdominant, and common chord progressions (like I-vi-IV-V)
to aid in memorization and composition.
