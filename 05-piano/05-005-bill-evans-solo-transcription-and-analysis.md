# Bill Evans Solo Transcription and Analysis

## Description
Transcribe a complete, complex jazz piano solo (e.g., Bill Evans on "Waltz for Debby" or "Autumn Leaves") entirely by ear. Notate it in software, analyze the harmonic choices (e.g., use of the melodic minor scale, drop-2 voicings), and learn to play it at tempo.
