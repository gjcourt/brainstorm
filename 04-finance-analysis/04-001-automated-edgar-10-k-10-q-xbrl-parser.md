# Automated EDGAR 10-K/10-Q XBRL Parser

## Description
Build a Go service that automatically downloads and parses XBRL data from the SEC EDGAR database. Extract key financial metrics (Revenue, Net Income, Free Cash Flow) from 10-K and 10-Q filings and store them in a structured PostgreSQL database.
