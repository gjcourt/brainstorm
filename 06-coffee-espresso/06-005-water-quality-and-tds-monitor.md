# Water Quality and TDS Monitor

## Description
Build an inline sensor for your espresso machine's water line (or reservoir) that continuously monitors the Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) and temperature of the water. Set up alerts in Home Assistant when the water quality drifts outside the ideal range for coffee extraction.
