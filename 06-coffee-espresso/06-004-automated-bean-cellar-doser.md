# Automated Bean Cellar Doser

## Description

Design and build a machine that automatically weighs out single doses of coffee beans (e.g., exactly
18.0g) into glass cellars. Use a stepper motor to drive an auger and a high-precision load cell to
stop the motor when the target weight is reached.
