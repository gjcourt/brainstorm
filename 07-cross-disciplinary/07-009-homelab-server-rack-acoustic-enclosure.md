# Homelab Server Rack Acoustic Enclosure

## Description

Build a custom wooden enclosure for your homelab servers that is specifically designed to deaden the
noise of high-RPM fans. Line the interior with mass-loaded vinyl and acoustic foam, and design a
baffled airflow path with ultra-quiet Noctua fans to maintain cooling.
