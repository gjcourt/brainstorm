# Woodworking Projects (15)

This category focuses on functional furniture, speaker cabinets, and homelab enclosures.

### 16. Custom Studio Monitor Speaker Cabinets
*   **Difficulty:** Hard
*   **Time Commitment:** Months
*   **Target Skills:** Acoustic Design, MDF/Plywood Joinery, Veneering
*   **Description:** Design and build a pair of 2-way studio monitors. Calculate the internal volume based on the Thiele/Small parameters of the drivers, build the cabinets with internal bracing, and apply a hardwood veneer finish.

### 17. Hardwood Homelab Server Rack
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** Mortise and Tenon Joinery, Rack Rail Installation
*   **Description:** Build a custom 12U or 18U server rack out of solid hardwood (e.g., Walnut or Cherry) to house your homelab equipment. Ensure proper ventilation and cable management routing.

### 18. Walnut Espresso Knock Box and Tamp Station
*   **Difficulty:** Easy
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-2 days
*   **Target Skills:** Box Making, Routing, Finishing (Food Safe)
*   **Description:** Create a custom knock box and tamping station for your Lucca A53 Mini. Use a solid piece of walnut, route out a recess for a stainless steel insert, and finish it with a food-safe oil/wax blend.

### 19. Adjustable Piano Bench with Storage
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** Upholstery, Hardware Installation, Leg Joinery
*   **Description:** Build a sturdy, adjustable-height piano bench. Incorporate a hidden storage compartment for sheet music and learn basic upholstery techniques for the seat cushion.

### 20. Modular Closet Organizer System
*   **Difficulty:** Hard
*   **Time Commitment:** Months
*   **Target Skills:** Cabinetry, Edge Banding, Drawer Slides
*   **Description:** Design and build a complete custom closet system using high-quality plywood. Include adjustable shelving, soft-close drawers, and hanging rods, maximizing the use of space.

### 21. Acoustic Diffuser Panels
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** Precision Cutting, Repetitive Assembly, Acoustic Theory
*   **Description:** Build a set of Quadratic Residue Diffusers (QRD) or Skyline diffusers for your listening room or studio. Calculate the well depths based on the target frequency range and assemble hundreds of wooden blocks.

### 22. Custom Mechanical Keyboard Wrist Rest
*   **Difficulty:** Easy
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-2 days
*   **Target Skills:** Shaping, Sanding, Finishing
*   **Description:** Craft a custom ergonomic wrist rest for your mechanical keyboard out of a single piece of exotic hardwood. Shape the angle for comfort and apply a durable polyurethane or oil finish.

### 23. Floating Shelves with Hidden LED Lighting
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** French Cleats, Routing, Basic Wiring
*   **Description:** Build a set of thick floating shelves using a torsion box construction or solid wood. Route a channel on the underside to embed an aluminum LED channel and diffuser for ambient lighting.

### 24. Japanese Toolbox for Electronics Tools
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** Hand Tool Joinery (Dovetails/Box Joints), Precision Fitting
*   **Description:** Build a traditional Japanese-style toolbox (Nomi-bako) to store your soldering iron, multimeter, and hand tools. Focus on tight-fitting joints without the use of metal fasteners.

### 25. Record Player Console Table
*   **Difficulty:** Hard
*   **Time Commitment:** Months
*   **Target Skills:** Furniture Design, Leg Tapers, Record Storage
*   **Description:** Design and build a mid-century modern inspired console table specifically for a turntable and amplifier. Include vertical dividers for vinyl record storage and hidden cable management.

### 26. Plywood Subwoofer Enclosure
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** Circle Routing, Port Tuning, Heavy Assembly
*   **Description:** Build a large, heavily braced subwoofer enclosure out of 3/4" Baltic Birch plywood. Calculate the port length and diameter for the desired tuning frequency and ensure the box is completely airtight.

### 27. Custom Router Table
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** T-Track Installation, Fence Construction, Lamination
*   **Description:** Build a custom router table extension for your table saw or a standalone unit. Laminate MDF for a dead-flat top, install an aluminum router lift plate, and build an adjustable split fence with dust collection.

### 28. Hardwood Cutting Board
*   **Difficulty:** Easy
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-2 days
*   **Target Skills:** Edge Gluing, Planing, Sanding
*   **Description:** A classic weekend project. Glue up strips of contrasting hardwoods (e.g., Maple, Walnut, Cherry) to create an edge-grain or end-grain cutting board. Finish with mineral oil and beeswax.

### 29. Mid-Century Modern Coffee Table
*   **Difficulty:** Hard
*   **Time Commitment:** Months
*   **Target Skills:** Angled Joinery, Tapered Legs, Complex Glue-ups
*   **Description:** Design and build a coffee table featuring iconic mid-century modern elements like splayed, tapered legs and a floating top. Focus on achieving perfect angles and seamless joints.

### 30. Wall-Mounted Tool French Cleat System
*   **Difficulty:** Medium
*   **Time Commitment:** 1-4 weeks
*   **Target Skills:** Shop Organization, Custom Tool Holders
*   **Description:** Outfit your workshop or garage with a French cleat wall system. Build custom, modular holders for your clamps, drills, hand planes, and measuring tools that can be easily rearranged.
