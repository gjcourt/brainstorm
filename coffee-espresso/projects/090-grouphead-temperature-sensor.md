# Grouphead Temperature Sensor

## Description
Install a highly responsive temperature sensor (like a PT100 RTD) directly on or inside the grouphead of your espresso machine. Monitor the temperature drop during a shot to understand the thermal stability of the system and adjust your flush routine accordingly.
