# ESP32 Espresso Shot Profiler and Logger

## Description
Build a standalone device that sits next to your espresso machine. Use a pressure transducer tapped into the grouphead and a load cell under the drip tray to log the pressure and weight of every shot in real-time, sending the data to your homelab for analysis.
